Tommy McHugh

Undergraduate Researcher

Tommy McHugh is a Learning Sciences student with a specialization in Human-Computer Interaction at Northwestern University. He studies the design of technologies for accessibility, learning, and creativity. Recently, his research has explored how to empower students and content professionals with disabilities to build their own assistive technologies and how to design inclusive computional learning experiences through the creation of accessible digital media.

Tommy has received an award by the ACM and Microsoft for his research and he has previously interned at Google Research and ï£¿ Apple. He currently works at the Northwestern Inclusive Technology Lab and is advised by Professor Anne Marie Piper. This work is currently funded in part by NSF Grant IIS-1901456.
